# What it's about

In this paper, we test the extent to which creativity performance depends on the specific task used, and whether this pattern generalizes across cultures.

# Abstract

The article examines task specificity in creativity by comparing performance patterns across cultural groups. Rather than assuming a single, task-invariant creativity trait, it evaluates how strongly outcomes depend on the task format and demands. Findings support nuanced interpretations of creativity scores and emphasize the need for multi-task assessment designs in cross-cultural work.
