A Meta-Analysis of the Relationship Between Intelligence and Different Measures of Creative Potential

By Nils Myszkowski in Meta-analysis Creativity Intelligence

PDF

What it’s about

In this paper, we examine a meta-analysis of the relationship between intelligence and different measures of creative potential.

Abstract

This paper examines the focal construct and reports empirical and/or psychometric evidence relevant to its interpretation. The findings contribute to the literature by clarifying measurement, theoretical implications, and practical applications in context.
